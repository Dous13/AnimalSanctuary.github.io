Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) section. Here, we aim to provide you with answers to the most common questions we receive about our Animal Sanctuary. Whether you are a new or returning customer, our FAQs will help you better understand our services and how we can help you find your bestfriend.

Your First Order!

Contact us through email or phone, we are happy to help you. Fill out your details to sign up. Once registration is complete, you can start booking your request for pick up.

We suggest you segregate the whites from the colored ones and label items that require hand washing, dry cleaning and ironing. A note for any special instruction in handling your clothes is ideal.

We aim to deliver your clothes after 3 days or earlier. If you prefer a same day/next day delivery, an extra fee shall be applied for express service. If you'd prefer later than the standard delivery schedule, just let us know when you want your clothes back.
Know The Basics

We offer machine wash, hand washing, dry cleaning, ironing service and pick up/delivery in nearby areas.

Should there be an issue with your laundry, call us immediately and we will make every effort to resolve the matter to your satisfaction. Please understand that some issues, like persistent stains, may not be able to be resolved regardless of available products and our efforts.

We encourage contactless payment to practice safe distancing. ONLINE PAYMENT For drop service delivery, payment must be made in advance.
