Why Adopt?

Adopting dogs or cats offers numerous benefits, enhancing both your life and theirs. These loyal companions provide unconditional love, companionship, and emotional support, promoting mental well-being. The act of adopting also contributes to reducing the population of homeless animals and alleviating the strain on overcrowded shelters. Dogs foster an active lifestyle through walks and play, promoting physical health. Cats, known for their independent nature, offer low-maintenance companionship, making them suitable for various lifestyles. The bond formed with a adopted pet creates a sense of responsibility and purpose. Ultimately, embracing the joy and fulfillment of adopting a dog or cat not only enriches your life but also saves theirs.

How Can I Help?

Adopting - taking one of our dogs into your family

Donating - support special projects, assist with running costs or simply buying some food

Sponsoring - regularly send money to look after a specific dog and receive updates

Fostering - looking after a dog temporarily while they recover or wait for adoption

Taking Action - look after stray dogs in your neighborhood with food and basic medicine

Volunteering and Fund-raising - lend us your time, talents and skills, or plan events and fund-raisers with your friends

Spreading the word - like and share our Facebook pages and this website with your friends and contacts
