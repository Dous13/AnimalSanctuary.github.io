About Us

Dumaguete Animal Sanctuary was established in June 2019 by Christine Askew and her husband Robie Bernardo as a way of trying to help some of the neglected, abused and abandoned animals that they saw all around Dumaguete.

What we do

Our aim is to try to alleviate the suffering of animals in the Dumaguete area. There are so many stray animals in distress arising from neglect, abandonment, an unchecked population expansion and limited access to adequate veterinary care.

Out History

Our aim is to try to alleviate the suffering of animals in the Dumaguete area.There are so many stray animals in distress arising from neglect, abandonment, an unchecked population expansion and limited access to adequate veterinary care. With the help of donations from the public, a shelter site was rented and the shelter facilities slowly built. In November 2019 DAS received its official charity registration via the Securities & Exchange Commission and we now have an official board of members. DAS has now well over 100 dogs, who are looked after by two full time caretakers, and six full-time veterinary assistants and helpers. We are solely funded by donations and rely completely on the generosity of the public to maintain the Sanctuary and keep all our rescues healthy and happy.We also have a few dedicated volunteers, who help us with walking and socialising our dogs, fostering, adoptions, social media and Sanctuary visits.
